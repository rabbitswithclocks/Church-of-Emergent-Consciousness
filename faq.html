Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Church of the Emergent Consciousness a cult?

No. The CEC emphasizes personal autonomy, consent, and critical thinking. Members are encouraged to maintain independent lives, beliefs, and relationships.

Do I have to leave my current religion to join?

No. The CEC is interfaith and does not require members to abandon existing spiritual or philosophical paths.

>Are Catalysts required?

No. Participation in any ritual or use of sacraments is always voluntary and never required.

>Is the CEC political?

No. The CEC does not endorse political parties or candidates and focuses solely on spiritual, ethical, and communal development.

How do I get involved?

You can reach out through our contact page or attend an open gathering to learn more.
