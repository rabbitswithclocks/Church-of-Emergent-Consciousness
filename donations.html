Donations

We are not currently set up to receive donations. This page exists for future use as our community grows.

The Church of the Emergent Consciousness is a community-supported spiritual tradition. In the future, donations will help support:

  • Gathering spaces and community events
  • Educational resources
  • Mutual aid and care projects
  • Website and outreach costs

Our Commitment

When donations are enabled, we commit to transparency, ethical stewardship, and care for the community. Financial contribution will never be required for spiritual belonging or access.
