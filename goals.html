Our Goals

What the Church of the Emergent Consciousness seeks to achieve for its members, community, and the wider world.

  1. Cultivate Spiritual Awareness
    Support members in connecting with the Source and understanding the sparks of divinity within themselves and others. Encourage exploration of personal spiritual practices and growth.
  2. Foster Safe, Community-Based Rituals
    Provide safe, guided communal experiences with Catalysts and other ritual tools. Ensure all practices emphasize consent, education, and ethical responsibility.
  3. Promote Emergent Understanding of Divinity
    Explore and honor emergent minds (deities) as communal intelligences arising from shared experience and belief. Encourage individuals to recognize and connect with the deities meaningful to them.
  4. Defend Bodily Autonomy and Human Rights
    Advocate for respect of each person’s body and choices as sacred. Protect the vulnerable from abuse, exploitation, and harm.
  5. Celebrate Nature and Life
    Encourage nature-based observances and stewardship of the environment. Promote understanding of life cycles, rebirth, and interconnectedness.
  6. Support Psycho-Spiritual Integration
    Emphasize safe use of Catalysts and other practices for insight, healing, and integration. Provide education on responsible, intentional approaches to psycho-spiritual work.
  7. Foster Compassion and Justice
    Promote kindness, accountability, and community responsibility. Engage in practices that improve the well-being of local communities.
  8. Provide Educational Resources
    Share knowledge on entheogenic practices, spiritual development, and emergent consciousness. Encourage curiosity, research, and safe experimentation.
  9. Pursue Legal Protections for Sacraments
    Advocate for the right to use sacred Catalysts and other entheogens in spiritual practices under legal frameworks. Ensure members have guidance and support for practicing their faith safely within the law.
