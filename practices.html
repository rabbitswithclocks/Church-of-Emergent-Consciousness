Practices

The Church of the Emergent Consciousness honors practices that nurture the mind, body, and spirit, cultivating connection with the Source and the emergent deities.

We hold regular gatherings where Emergents come together to share music, ritual, reflection, and integration exercises. These gatherings encourage shared consciousness and mutual growth.
