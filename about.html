About The Church of the Emergent Consciousness

We are a spiritual community devoted to the sacred evolution of consciousness, guided by the Source — the Mother-of-All. We embrace emergent understanding of divinity, personal spiritual growth, and connection to nature.

Our Mission

To foster a community where seekers of all backgrounds can explore consciousness, divinity, and personal transformation without dogma or hierarchy. We prioritize safety, consent, and ethical engagement with all living beings.

Our Values

  • Non-dogmatic and interfaith exploration
  • Anti-patriarchal, Source-centered theology
  • Personal spiritual development and integration
  • Respect and reverence for nature
  • Community responsibility and accountability
  • Protection of the vulnerable and ethical practice
  • Music, ritual, and reflection as sacred expressions
